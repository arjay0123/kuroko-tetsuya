About Tetsuya Kuroko

Tetsuya Kuroko is the main protagonist of the anime "Kuroko no Basuke" (Kuroko's Basketball). He is a former member of the "Generation of Miracles" and is known for his ability to play invisibly on the court, using misdirection to pass the ball and support his team.

Kuroko's dream is to make his new team, Seirin High, the best in Japan and defeat his former teammates from the Generation of Miracles.
